This folder contains:

Account_List.xlsx contains a list of your financial accounts (your chart of accounts).
Potential information captured: name, type, and balance for each account.

Balance_Sheet_Summary.AllDates.xlsx contains a summary of what you own (assets), what you owe (liabilities), and what you invested (equity).

Trial_Balance.AllDates.xlsx contains a summary of the debit and credit balances of each account on your chart of accounts during a period of time.

General_Ledger.<Date Range>.xlsx contains the beginning balance, transactions, and total for each account in your chart of accounts.

Journal.<Date Range>.xlsx contains the debits and credits for each transaction, listed by date.

Profit_and_Loss.AllDates.xlsx contains your income, expenses, and net income (profit or loss). Also called an income statement.

Customer_Contact_List.<Page Number>.xlsx contains a list of your customers.
Potential information captured: phone number, email, billing address, and other contact info for each customer.

Employee_Contact_List.<Page Number>.xlsx contains a list of your employees.
Potential information captured: phone number, email, and other contact info for each employee.

Vendor_Contact_List.<Page Number>.xlsx contains a list of your vendors.
Potential information captured: name, company, phone number, email, mailing address, and account number for each vendor.

Product_Service_List.<Page Number>.xlsx contains your products and services you sell or purchase.
Potential information captured: sales price, name, description, and (optionally) purchase cost and quantity on hand.
